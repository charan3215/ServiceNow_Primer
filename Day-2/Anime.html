Naruto Shippuden

created by Masashi Kishimoto

Description

Naruto: Shippuden is an anime television series mainly adapted from Part II of Masashi Kishimoto's original Naruto manga series, with exactly 500 episodes. It is set two and a half years after the original series in the Naruto universe, following the ninja teenager Naruto Uzumaki and his allies.

Episodes Info

Homecoming

A figure passes through the gates. It's an older Naruto, who has returned from a long training journey with Jiraiya. Naruto Uzumaki is back!

The Akatsuki Makes Its Move

Naruto and Sakura team up and challenge Kakashi to a survival challenge to show off their progress

The Results of Training

Naruto's and Sakura's combined skills force Kakashi to use his Sharingan right off the bat. Naruto masterfully unleashes his jutsu one after another.

The Jinchuriki of the Sand

Gaara is quick to detect Deidara's presence, and an aerial battle ensues with Deidara on his giant bird and Gaara atop sand.

The Kazekage Stands Tall

Naruto and Sakura defeat Kakashi in the survival challenge. Tsunade assigns them to a three-man squad led by Kakashi, who tells them they are now his equals.

Mission Cleared

In order to protect his village from Deidara's explosive clay, Gaara uses his chakra to create a giant sand shield.

Run, Kankuro

Gaara, his strength completely exhausted, is whisked away by Deidara. Kankuro ignores Baki's efforts to stop him and immediately sets out in pursuit with a platoon to rescue Gaara.
